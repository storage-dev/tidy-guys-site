Frequently Asked

Quick answers

What kind of cleaning does Tidy Guys handle?

Tidy Guys is a commercial cleaning company. We handle offices, medical and dental clinics, retail and restaurants, post-construction, post-event, and move-in / move-out cleans for property managers, tenants, and contractors across Nashville and Davidson County.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We're fully insured and can provide a Certificate of Insurance to your property manager or facility owner before service begins.

How quickly can you start?

Most commercial accounts can start within 5–7 business days of the walk-through. Urgent post-construction and post-event jobs can be scheduled faster — call (615) 955-TIDY.

What does pricing look like?

Commercial cleaning is priced per facility based on square footage, scope, and frequency. We give written quotes after a brief walk-through (in person or virtual) — no obligation.

Can you clean after hours or on weekends?

Yes. Most of our accounts are cleaned in the evening, overnight, or on weekends so your team and customers don't see us.
