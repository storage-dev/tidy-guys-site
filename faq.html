About Tidy Guys

What kind of cleaning does Tidy Guys handle?

Tidy Guys is a commercial cleaning company in Nashville. We handle offices, medical and dental clinics, retail and restaurants, post-construction, post-event, and move-in / move-out cleans for property managers, tenants, and contractors across Nashville and Davidson County.

Is Tidy Guys owner-led?

Yes. Tidy Guys was founded in 2021 in East Nashville and is still owner-led. The owner personally walks every new account before service begins and is the direct point of contact for facility managers and business owners — not a regional rep.

What areas does Tidy Guys serve?

We serve all of Nashville and Davidson County: East Nashville, Downtown, Germantown, the Gulch, Green Hills, 12 South, Berry Hill, Inglewood, plus Brentwood, Franklin, Hendersonville, Mt. Juliet, Antioch, Madison, and surrounding metro areas. See full service area →

Do you do residential house cleaning?

Tidy Guys is focused on commercial cleaning. We do offer move-in and move-out cleans for residential transitions, but we are not a recurring residential maid service.

Pricing & Quotes

How much does commercial cleaning cost in Nashville?

Commercial cleaning is priced per facility based on square footage, scope of work, frequency, and access. We give written quotes after a brief walk-through — no obligation. Most small offices fall into a predictable per-visit range, and we offer recurring discounts for weekly and daily accounts.

How fast can Tidy Guys provide a quote?

Most commercial quotes are returned within one business day after a brief walk-through or virtual consultation. Urgent post-construction and post-event jobs can be quoted same-day.

How do I get a quote?

Three ways: call (615) 955-TIDY, email info@mytidyguys.com, or fill out the quote form. Most quotes are returned within one business day.

Do you charge for the walk-through?

No. The walk-through and quote are always free. We don't charge until we clean.

Scheduling & Service

How quickly can you start service?

Most recurring commercial accounts can start within 5–7 business days of the walk-through. Urgent post-construction, post-event, and move-out jobs can often be scheduled within 24–72 hours.

Can you clean after hours or on weekends?

Yes. Most of our commercial accounts are cleaned in the evening, overnight, or on weekends so your team and customers don't see us. Tell us your preferred service window in your quote request.

Do you offer one-time cleans or only recurring?

Both. We handle one-time deep cleans, post-event cleanups, post-construction punch lists, and move-in / move-out turnovers. We also run recurring daily, weekly, and bi-weekly programs for offices, clinics, and property management accounts.

How do I know what's included in my cleaning?

Every account starts with a written scope of work — a checklist of exactly what gets cleaned and how often. You'll know exactly what's included before service begins, and you can change it at any time.

Trust, Safety & Insurance

Is Tidy Guys insured?

Yes. Tidy Guys is fully insured. A Certificate of Insurance can be issued to your property manager or facility owner before service begins.

Are your crews background-checked?

Yes. Every Tidy Guys crew member is background-checked and trained on our scope of work and safety protocols before they enter a client facility.

What if something gets missed during a clean?

Call us. Under our 100% satisfaction guarantee, we'll return and re-clean the missed item the same day or the next business morning at no additional charge.

Products, Equipment & Specialties

Do you supply your own products and equipment?

Yes. Tidy Guys brings all chemicals, equipment, and supplies. Eco-friendly and CDC-aligned products are available on request — important for medical, dental, and food-service spaces.

Do you handle floor stripping and waxing?

Yes. Hard-floor strip-and-wax, VCT care, polished concrete, and carpet shampooing are part of our janitorial program. We can include them in a recurring schedule or quote them as one-time projects.

Do you handle medical and dental facilities?

Yes. We service medical clinics, dental practices, and urgent-care offices using hospital-grade disinfectants, color-coded equipment to prevent cross-contamination, and HIPAA-aware crews. See medical cleaning →

Do you offer post-construction cleaning?

Yes. Tidy Guys provides rough cleans, final cleans, and touch-up cleans for general contractors, developers, and renovation projects across Nashville. See post-construction →

Do you handle move-in or move-out cleaning?

Yes. Office moves, suite turnovers, retail handoffs, and tenant transitions across the Nashville metro. Walls, baseboards, floors, kitchens, restrooms — every surface in an empty space. See move-in / move-out →

Do you offer post-event cleaning?

Yes. Weddings, corporate events, conferences, pop-ups, and venue rentals across Nashville. Same-night or next-morning turnaround. See post-event →
