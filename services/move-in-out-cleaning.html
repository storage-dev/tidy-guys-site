Move-in / move-out cleaning that stops the security-deposit fight

The hardest cleans are the empty ones. With nothing in the way, every surface is exposed — every baseboard, every cabinet interior, every smudge on every wall. The next tenant sees all of it. Tidy Guys handles move-in and move-out cleaning across Nashville for offices, retail, and commercial spaces in transition.

When this service is for you

  • Tenants moving out who need a clean space to recover their security deposit
  • Property managers turning over a suite between tenants
  • Office relocations where the outgoing space needs a final clean and the new space needs a fresh start
  • Retail handoffs between brands or pop-up cycles
  • Restaurant and bar transitions at lease end
  • Building owners staging spaces for showings or photography
  • Move-in cleans for tenants taking new space and wanting a clean baseline

What's included in a move-in / move-out clean

  • Walls — scuff mark removal, full wipe-down, spot wash where needed
  • Baseboards & trim — hand-wiped, every linear foot
  • Doors & door frames — including handles, hinges, and the tops where dust hides
  • Floors — vacuum carpet (steam clean if scoped), sweep and deep-mop hard floors
  • Cabinets & drawers — interior and exterior; wipe shelves, vacuum drawers
  • Kitchen / break room — fridge interior and exterior, microwave, sink, faucet, counters
  • Restrooms — full deep clean, descale, disinfect, restock
  • Windows & glass — interior glass, partitions, mirrors
  • Light fixtures & vents — dust off, wipe, replace covers as needed
  • Switches, outlets, thermostats — wipe down, disinfect
  • Trash & debris — leftover packing material, hangers, debris haul

Why move-in / move-out is its own service

Move-in / move-out cleaning is more thorough than a standard recurring clean and faster than a post-construction clean. The space is empty, which means we can hit every surface — but it also means the standards are higher. There is nowhere for dust to hide. Pricing reflects that: it's a project price, not a per-visit rate.

Pricing

Move-in / move-out cleans are priced as one-time projects based on square footage, the condition of the space, and any added services (carpet steam clean, window exterior, paint touch-up coordination). We give written quotes within one business day. Most jobs can be scheduled within 24–72 hours of quote acceptance.

Common scenarios we handle

  • Office tenant exiting a suite — full clean to satisfy lease turnover requirements
  • New office tenant taking possession — fresh clean before furniture install
  • Property manager turning a suite — between tenants, on the clock
  • Retail brand exiting a storefront — clean to landlord standards
  • Restaurant lease end — final clean before equipment removal or post-removal
  • Buyer closing on a building — pre-occupancy clean of common areas and tenant suites
