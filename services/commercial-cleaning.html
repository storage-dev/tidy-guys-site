Commercial cleaning that actually stays consistent

Most Nashville businesses don't switch cleaners because they want to. They switch because the company they hired stopped showing up the same way every visit. The bathroom looks great in week one, and by month three the trash is overflowing and nobody knows who to call.

Tidy Guys is built around fixing that. Every commercial account starts with a written scope of work, gets the same trained crew on a fixed schedule, and reports up to one accountable point of contact — Leo Knott, the owner.

What's included in a commercial cleaning program

Every program is custom-built around your facility, but most include some combination of:

  • Daily, weekly, or custom-frequency visits — we work around your operations
  • Trash removal and liner replacement — every bin, every visit
  • Restroom service — clean, sanitize, restock paper, soap, sanitizer
  • Kitchen and break room — counters, sinks, fridge exterior, microwaves, dishwashing if requested
  • Floor care — vacuum carpets, sweep and mop hard floors
  • Glass and entryway — front-of-house first impression
  • Dusting and surface wiping — desks, conference rooms, lobbies, reception
  • Disinfection of high-touch points — door handles, light switches, shared tools
  • Periodic deep cleans — windows, baseboards, vents, light fixtures, scheduled monthly or quarterly
  • Floor stripping & waxing, carpet shampoo — added to recurring schedule or quoted as one-time

How pricing works

Commercial cleaning is priced per facility based on square footage, scope of work, frequency, and access. After a brief walk-through (in person or virtual), we send a written quote — usually within one business day. Recurring accounts get a per-visit rate. One-time deep cleans, post-construction, post-event, and move-in/move-out work are quoted as projects.

Walk-throughs and quotes are always free. We don't charge until we clean.

How we keep cleaning consistent over time

  • Documented scope of work — what gets cleaned, how often, by whom
  • Same crew every visit, with backup coverage planned in advance
  • Recurring visit reports so you know what was done and when
  • One accountable point of contact — the owner
  • 100% satisfaction guarantee — if something gets missed, we re-clean it free
