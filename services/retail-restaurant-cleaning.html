Retail & Restaurant Cleaning in Nashville, TN

Cleaning that protects your customer experience

Retail and restaurants live and die by the customer's first thirty seconds. The smudged front door, the dusty shelf, the sticky tabletop — every detail either reinforces the brand or undermines it. Tidy Guys cleans Nashville storefronts, boutiques, restaurants, and bars after hours so your team opens to a clean space every shift.

Retail cleaning

  • Front of house — sales floor vacuum and mop, fitting rooms, mirrors, glass, counters, point-of-sale
  • Window and glass — exterior and interior glass, doors, partitions
  • Restrooms — clean, disinfect, restock
  • Stockroom & back areas — sweep, mop, trash haul
  • Display and fixture care — dusting, surface wipe (we do not move merchandise unless requested)
  • Trash & recycling — interior to outdoor receptacles

Restaurant & bar cleaning

  • Dining room — sweep, mop, sanitize tables, chairs, booths, banquettes, host stand, menus
  • Bar areas — bar top, back bar, bar floor, glass, brass and chrome polish
  • Restrooms — heavy-traffic clean, disinfect, restock; periodic deep cleans
  • Front entry — glass, door hardware, entryway mat care
  • Kitchen support cleaning — floors, walls, hood exterior, prep surfaces (we coordinate around your hood-cleaning vendor)
  • Trash & recycling — interior to dumpster, including grease bin areas
  • Periodic deep cleans — vents, fixtures, baseboards, behind-equipment cleans

Why we clean Nashville hospitality so well

  • We clean overnight or pre-open so your guests never see us
  • We coordinate with hood-cleaning, pest control, and other facility vendors
  • Crews are trained on health-code-relevant cleaning standards
  • Fast response when someone calls about a missed surface — same-day or next-morning fix
  • Owner-led — the owner walks every account before service starts
