Office cleaning that doesn't disrupt your team

The best office cleaning is the kind nobody notices is happening. Trash bins are empty in the morning. The kitchen smells fresh. The conference rooms are reset. The bathrooms are stocked. And nobody had to chase anyone down to make it happen.

Tidy Guys runs nightly and weekly office cleaning programs for businesses across Nashville. Every account starts with a written scope of work, gets the same crew every visit, and reports up to one accountable owner.

What's included in office cleaning

  • Trash & recycling — empty all bins, replace liners, haul to dumpster or chute
  • Restrooms — clean and disinfect toilets, urinals, sinks, mirrors, partitions; restock paper, soap, and sanitizer
  • Kitchens & break rooms — wipe counters, sinks, microwaves, fridge exterior, tables, chairs; load/run dishwasher if requested
  • Floors — vacuum carpets, sweep and mop hard floors, spot-clean spills
  • Glass & entryways — clean lobby glass, doors, partitions, conference room walls
  • Dusting & surface wiping — desks, conference tables, lobby furniture, reception
  • High-touch disinfection — door handles, light switches, elevator buttons, shared keyboards on request
  • Stocking & supplies — paper goods, soap, sanitizer (we can manage and bill, or your supplies)
  • Periodic deep cleans — windows, baseboards, vents, fixtures, scheduled monthly or quarterly

How office cleaning is priced

Office cleaning in Nashville is priced per visit based on square footage, the scope of work, frequency, and access. Most office accounts come in on a recurring weekly, 3×-weekly, or daily cadence with a per-visit rate. We offer recurring discounts for higher frequency.

Walk-throughs and quotes are free. Most quotes are returned within one business day.

Who we run office cleaning for

Office suites, coworking spaces, corporate floors, multi-tenant buildings, professional services firms (legal, accounting, finance), creative agencies, software companies, and any office that wants a cleaner that actually shows up the same way every visit.

After-hours, overnight, or weekend

We clean offices in the evening, overnight, or on weekends so your team and clients never see us. Tell us your preferred service window in your quote request and we'll work around it.
