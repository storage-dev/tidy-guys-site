Medical cleaning that respects what your space does

Patients judge a clinic the moment they walk in. A clean waiting room signals safe care. A spotless restroom signals attention to detail. And in a clinical setting, cleanliness is not just optics — it is the operational foundation of trust.

Tidy Guys provides medical and dental cleaning across the Nashville metro, built around CDC-aligned protocols, hospital-grade disinfectants, color-coded equipment to prevent cross-contamination, and crews trained to be HIPAA-aware on every visit.

What medical cleaning includes

  • Reception & waiting rooms — full disinfection of high-touch surfaces, chairs, counters, doors, magazines (or removal)
  • Exam rooms — surfaces, sinks, exam tables, stools, computers, light fixtures, floor
  • Operatories (dental) — surfaces, suction lines (when access permitted), trim, floor; disinfection between deep-cleans
  • Restrooms — clinical-grade disinfection, restocking, fixture detail
  • Sterilization & lab areas — surfaces and floors only; we never disturb sterile equipment or autoclaves
  • Break rooms & staff areas — full clean, disinfection, restocking
  • Hallways & common areas — vacuum, mop, disinfect handrails and door hardware
  • Glass & entryways — first impression matters in clinical spaces
  • Trash & bio handling — we follow your bio-waste protocol; we do not handle red-bag waste unless contracted

Practices we clean

  • Family medicine and primary care
  • Dental practices and orthodontics
  • Pediatric clinics
  • Urgent care
  • Specialty clinics (dermatology, ENT, orthopedics, etc.)
  • Mental health and counseling practices
  • Chiropractic and physical therapy
  • Veterinary clinics (with appropriate scope)
  • Med spas and aesthetic practices

Color-coded cleaning, no cross-contamination

Our crews use color-coded microfiber cloths and mop heads that never cross zones — restrooms get one color, exam rooms another, common areas another. Equipment is laundered and rotated, not reused dirty. This is a small detail that matters a lot in clinical settings.

HIPAA-aware crews

Our crews are trained to keep their eyes on the floor and the surfaces, not the charts. We do not photograph rooms with patient information visible, do not move paperwork, and do not enter rooms marked private without authorization.

Pricing

Medical cleaning is priced per facility based on square footage, the number of operatories or exam rooms, frequency, and scope. Most practices run on a daily or 3×-weekly schedule. Free walk-through, written quote within one business day.
