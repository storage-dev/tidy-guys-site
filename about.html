Founded in Nashville. Run by the owner.

Tidy Guys was founded in 2021 in Nashville. What started as a small crew handling offices and post-construction jobs has grown into a full-service commercial cleaning company serving the Nashville metro and Davidson County.

The reason facility managers call us back is simple: the owner answers the phone. Most national cleaning chains rotate crews and hide behind regional managers. We do the opposite — same crew, same schedule, same person accountable. The owner personally walks every new account before service begins.

What we believe about commercial cleaning

  • Cleanliness is operational. A clean office is faster, healthier, and easier to lease. A clean clinic gets better Yelp reviews. A clean restaurant passes inspections.
  • Consistency beats perfection. We'd rather show up the same way 200 nights in a row than have one spectacular clean and three mediocre ones.
  • Document everything. Every account starts with a written scope of work. Every visit produces a record. No mysteries about what happened.
  • The owner shows up. Big problem? Small problem? You get the owner on the phone — not a call center.

Who we serve

Tidy Guys is built for Nashville's commercial mix: offices and coworking spaces, medical and dental clinics, retail stores and restaurants, music and event venues, post-construction sites, property management companies, and tenants doing move-in or move-out cleans across the metro.

We are not a residential maid service. If you need a one-time house clean, we can refer you to one of the local Nashville companies that specializes there. If you run a business, manage a building, or build buildings — we're the right call.
